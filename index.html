About Me

I am a passionate and dedicated VFX artist with over 10 years of experience in the industry. My journey began with an obsession with VFX breakdowns and has evolved into a career creating seamless and impactful visual effects for a variety of projects. My expertise lies in keying, paintouts, and blending photo-realistic digital assets. I am always looking for new challenges and collaborative opportunities to push the boundaries of what's possible.

I am proficient in software such as Nuke, Mocha, DaVinci Resolve, and Blender.
